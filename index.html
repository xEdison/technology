TECHNOLOGY

Technology encompasses the set of knowledge, tools, and processes used to create, adapt, and utilize tools, machines, and systems to solve problems and meet human needs. From the earliest stone tools to the digital age, technology has been a fundamental driving force in the evolution of society. It has revolutionized how we communicate, work, travel, learn, and entertain ourselves. Additionally, it has brought innovations in fields such as medicine, agriculture, energy, and the environment, helping address complex challenges and improve quality of life worldwide. Technology continues to advance at a rapid pace, creating new opportunities and challenges as we move into the future.
